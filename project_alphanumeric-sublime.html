Alphanumeric Sublime

[2020]

The information age has rendered traditional conceptions of the sublime obsolete. No place is unknowable with Google Earth, and our phones nullify most terrors of a vast, untamed wilderness. Alphanumeric Sublime cheekily comments on our mediated relationship with nature, generating paintings of the sublime as alphanumeric character mosaics.
